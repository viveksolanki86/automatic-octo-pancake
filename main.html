Blackjack Rules

  • Each player is dealt two cards.
  • Cards 2-10 are worth their face value.
  • J, Q, K are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11.
  • Goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Player can "Hit" to take more cards or "Stand" to stop.
  • Dealer must hit until 17 or more.
  • Blackjack (A + 10 card) beats all other 21s.
